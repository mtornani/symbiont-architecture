The Substrate
BitNet: the ternary architecture

Microsoft BitNet b1.58: weights as three values {-1, 0, +1}. Runs on CPU. 0.4GB. 12x more efficient.

It's not a small LLM. It's the natural structure of a nervous system. Biological neurons: excite (+1), silence (0), inhibit (-1).

SAMs are naturally ternary. The primitive response to a stimulus: react, don't react, withdraw. Three values. BitNet is the hardware substrate of the synthetic organism.
